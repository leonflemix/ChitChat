Configuration Needed

This app requires environment variables to connect to Firebase.

1. Go to your project settings on Vercel.

2. Navigate to the "Environment Variables" section.

3. Add the six required variables (e.g., `VITE_API_KEY`, `VITE_AUTH_DOMAIN`, etc.) with their corresponding values from your Firebase project.

After adding the variables, you must redeploy your project for the changes to apply.
